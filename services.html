Service packages

Choose the right entry point for your RF challenge.

Start with a focused package that gives you a clear technical path. Each service can stand alone or become the first step toward a larger design, measurement, or debugging engagement.

Build

RF Design

For teams that need hands-on RF, microwave, antenna, or mmWave design support before prototype fabrication or system integration.

  • RF architecture and frequency-plan support
  • Front-end, amplifier, antenna, or matching-network design guidance
  • Layout-aware recommendations for high-frequency performance
  • Simulation, measurement planning, and design-risk reduction
  • Prototype-readiness review before fabrication
Review

RF Design Review Sprint

A focused expert review for early-stage designs, proposals, test plans, or teams that need a second technical opinion before committing more resources.

  • 90-minute technical review session
  • Review of goals, schematics, layout, packaging, and test assumptions
  • Identification of high-risk RF design choices
  • Written risk list and improvement roadmap
  • Recommended measurement and validation plan
Measure

Antenna and mmWave Measurement Sprint

Measurement and reporting for antennas, wireless devices, RIS prototypes, high-frequency boards, and advanced communication or sensing systems.

  • S-parameters, return loss, and frequency response
  • Radiation pattern, gain, beam behavior, and directional performance
  • 2D or 3D plots where applicable
  • Measurement-backed interpretation of performance limits
  • Short technical report with actionable recommendations
Debug

RF Failure-Debug Sprint

For prototypes that are not meeting range, gain, efficiency, stability, linearity, noise, matching, or measurement expectations.

  • Root-cause measurement strategy
  • Amplifier, antenna, board, packaging, or device-level analysis
  • Comparison of expected behavior versus measured behavior
  • Prioritized fixes and retest recommendations
  • Optional follow-on redesign and validation support
How we work

Simple process. Serious technical depth.

The first step is intentionally small: define the device, frequency range, current challenge, and decision you need to make. RFSpectra then recommends the simplest service package that can produce useful evidence.

1. Discovery

Share the device type, operating frequency, target performance, current measurements, drawings, and deadline.

2. Scope

RFSpectra recommends a focused design, review, measurement, or debug package with clear deliverables.

3. Deliver

You receive measured results, technical interpretation, design guidance, and practical next steps.
